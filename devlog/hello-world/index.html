2026-04-02 Update

Hello, World

Welcome to the Carrier Wave devlog. We're starting this space to share what we're building, why we're building it, and the occasional deep-dive into the decisions behind the app.

Carrier Wave has been growing steadily since launch, and we've heard from a lot of operators who want to know more about what's coming, how things work under the hood, and the thinking behind the features they use every day. This devlog is our answer to that.

What to expect

We'll use this space for a mix of things:

  • Release notes and commentary on new versions
  • Behind-the-scenes looks at how features are designed and built
  • Deep-dives into the technical decisions that shape the app
  • Previews of what we're working on next

Posts won't follow a fixed schedule. We'll write when we have something worth sharing rather than posting on a calendar. Quality over cadence.

Stay in the loop

The best way to follow along is to check back here, or join us on Discord where we share updates and chat with operators directly.

73 de the Carrier Wave team.
